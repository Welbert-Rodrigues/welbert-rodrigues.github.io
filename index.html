No Dia da Sessão

  • Alimente-se bem e hidrate-se
  • Evite álcool na véspera
  • Use roupas que facilitem o acesso à área

Cuidados Pós-Tatuagem

  • Lave com sabão neutro e água fria
  • Aplique a pomada ou creme cicatrizante indicado
  • Evite sol, mar, piscinas e sauna nas primeiras semanas
  • Evite musculação e outros exercícios físicos de maior impacto
  • Não coce ou retire as casquinhas

Qualquer dúvida, estou à disposição :)

Booking and Deposit

A deposit of 30% of the total price is required to confirm your appointment. This amount will be deducted from the total tattoo cost. The deposit is non-refundable. If you cancel on the day of the session, a rescheduling fee of 10% of the total tattoo cost will be charged.

Waitlist

When I open my schedule for other states or countries, I start the pricing and booking process for those locations. Once dates are confirmed, I notify clients on the list in advance, ensuring priority in choosing their appointment time.

To book, a deposit of 50% of the total price is required. Due to limited availability, cancellations without prior notice will result in a 20% fee of the total price for rescheduling, subject to availability.

On the Day of the Session

  • Eat well and stay hydrated
  • Avoid alcohol the day before
  • Wear clothing that allows easy access to the area

Post-Tattoo Care

  • Wash with neutral soap and cold water
  • Apply the recommended healing ointment or cream
  • Avoid sun, sea, pools, and saunas for the first few weeks
  • Avoid weightlifting and high-impact exercises
  • Do not scratch or remove scabs
