Booking and Deposit

A 30% deposit is required to secure your appointment. This amount will be deducted from the total cost of your tattoo. The deposit is non-refundable. If you cancel on the day of your session, a 10% rescheduling fee will be charged based on the total tattoo cost.

Waitlist

When I open my schedule for other states or countries, I begin the booking process for those locations. Once dates are confirmed, I notify clients on the waitlist in advance, giving them priority in choosing their appointment time.

To confirm your appointment, a 50% deposit is required. Due to limited availability, cancellations without notice will incur a 20% rescheduling fee, depending on availability.

On the Day of Your Session

  • Eat well and stay hydrated
  • Avoid alcohol the day before
  • Wear clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area

Post-Tattoo Care

  • Wash with neutral soap and cold water
  • Apply the recommended healing ointment or cream
  • Avoid sun, sea, pools, and saunas for the first few weeks
  • Avoid weightlifting and high-impact exercises
  • Do not scratch or remove scabs
